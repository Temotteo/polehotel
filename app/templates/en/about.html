About Pole Hotel

Who We Are

Pole Hotel is a hospitality unit dedicated to delivering integrated solutions in accommodation, events, restaurant and catering services, guided by high standards of quality, professionalism and attention to detail.

Strategically located in the centre of Marracuene Town, on Avenida Cardeal Dom Alexandre dos Santos, S/N, Bairro do Cajual (Maputo Circular Road), Pole Hotel offers convenience and easy access for both local and international guests.

The hotel is owned by JUSA Investimentos, Lda., a private limited company duly incorporated under Mozambican law and published in the Official Gazette (Boletim da República), III Series, No. 87 of 30 October 2014.

Our facilities are modern, functional and versatile, designed to meet the needs of companies, public and private institutions and individual clients, ensuring comfort, operational efficiency and service excellence.

Our Values

  • ✔ Professionalism and respect for our clients

We value the continuous development of our staff, teamwork and the constant improvement of our services, fostering transparent, long-term and trustworthy relationships with partners and institutions.

Our Mission

The mission of Pole Hotel is to provide integrated hospitality, restaurant, events and catering solutions, delivering comfortable, efficient and personalised experiences.

Our services include hotel accommodation, organisation of seminars and conferences, business and leisure retreats, catering and buffet services with capacity to serve over 300 meals, as well as the organisation of events both on and off the hotel premises, transfer services, vehicle rental and other related services.

Our Services

Accommodation (29 rooms)

Pole Hotel offers 29 comfortable and fully equipped rooms, distributed across the following categories:

  • Standard Twin Room
  • Standard Double Room
  • Economy Double Room
  • Executive Room
  • Special Executive Room

Accommodation rates include a full breakfast, with a variety of options ranging from fresh fruit to hot dishes. All rooms provide free Wi-Fi internet access, with additional services available upon request, such as laundry services.

Conference Facilities
  • Executive Meeting Room – capacity up to 12 guests
  • Training / Meeting Room – capacity up to 35 guests
Events Tent

Pole Hotel features an events tent with capacity for up to 350 guests, suitable for conferences, ceremonies, product launches and corporate or social events.

Complete event packages are available, including decoration, tables, chairs, tableware, sound system, DJ, sound technician, wait staff and support from a dedicated event manager.

Restaurant

Our restaurant is open to both hotel guests and external clients, offering quality meals in a welcoming environment, with high standards of presentation and customer service.

Catering Services

We provide tailored catering services for corporate and non-corporate events, adapted to the specific needs of each client and event format.

Why Choose Pole Hotel?

Pole Hotel stands out for its integrated service approach, combining accommodation, catering, event organisation and complementary services under one roof.

This approach allows our clients to benefit from operational efficiency, cost optimisation, consistent quality and a single, reliable service partner for multiple needs.

We remain at your disposal for a presentation meeting or for the preparation of customised proposals, according to your organisation’s specific requirements.
